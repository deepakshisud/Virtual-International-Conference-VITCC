Publication Policy
  • Any manuscript submitted to VICFCNT-2021 must be original and the content thereof, or substantial parts of it, must not be under consideration / published by any other journal.
    		•
  • Minimum length of the paper is 4 pages and maximum length of the paper is 8 pages.
    		•
  • All submissions to VICFCNT-2021 are assessed by the chairs of the respective tracks, who will decide whether they are suitable for peer review based on:
    • The similarity index of a professional plagiarism checker, which should be less than 15%.
    • The timeliness, the interest and importance of the topic.
    		•
  • The peer review process involves the review of the manuscripts by two independent reviewers.
    		•
  • Technical committee will make a decision on the acceptance of the manuscript for publication based on the reviewers’ reports and the reports will be sent to the authors for suitable modification.
    		•
  • Only the papers that are presented in the conference will be considered for publication.
    		•
  • Publishing in journals will take a minimum of 3 months to 6 months from the date of the conference.
    		•
  • It is planned to publish the peer reviewed and selected papers of conference as proceedings either with
    • Springer in their prestigious “Lecture Notes in Electrical Engineering”series (https://www.springer.com/series/7818) (Scopus indexed). For detailed instructions for authors of conference proceedings, kindly visit the following link: https://www.springer.com/us/authors-editors/conference- proceedings..

      • OR

    • IOP in their prestigious “Journal of Physics: Conference Series (JPCS)”(https://iopscience.iop.org/journal/1742-6596) (EI Compendex, Scopus Indexed). For Detailed instruction for authors of conference proceedings, kindly visit the following link https://publishingsupport.iopscience.iop.org/author-guidelines-for-conference-proceedings/
